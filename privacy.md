# Liminal Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** 11 April 2026

Liminal is a wellness app that helps you wake, rest, meditate, and chant with intention. This policy explains what happens to your information when you use it. The short version: nothing leaves your phone.

## What we collect

Nothing. Liminal has no accounts, no servers, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking. We do not see your data because we never receive it.

## What stays on your device

The app stores the following locally on your iPhone, using the operating system's standard on-device storage (AsyncStorage), with keys prefixed `@liminal_`:

- Your first name (entered during onboarding)
- The text and audio recording of your Sankalpa (your personal intention)
- Your wake alarm time and notification preferences
- Your session history for Meditate, Chant, and Rest, including streaks and plan progress
- App settings

This information never leaves your device. You can erase all of it at any time from **Settings → Data & Privacy** inside the app, or by deleting the app.

## Microphone

Liminal asks for microphone access for one reason: so you can record your Sankalpa in your own voice. The recording is saved as an audio file on your device and played back to you during practice. It is never uploaded, shared, transmitted, or analysed by us or anyone else. You can delete the recording at any time from Settings.

## Notifications

Liminal uses local notifications for your wake alarm and optional practice reminders. These are scheduled directly by your iPhone — there is no push notification server, and we do not see when or whether you receive them. You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings or inside Liminal.

## Third parties

Liminal does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, crash reporters that collect personal data, or any other third-party services that gather information about you. The app's audio assets (singing bowls, dawn chorus, brainwave tones) are bundled into the app itself — no streaming, no network calls.

## Children

Liminal is a general-audience wellness app and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone — children included — because we do not collect information at all.

## Security

Because your data lives only on your device, its security is governed by your iPhone's built-in protections (passcode, Face ID / Touch ID, full-disk encryption). We recommend keeping your device's operating system up to date.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at the same URL with a new effective date. Material changes will also be noted in the app's release notes.
