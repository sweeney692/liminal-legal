## Common questions

### The app isn't waking me up
Liminal uses local notifications scheduled through iOS. For the wake sequence to run:
- Notifications must be enabled for Liminal (iOS Settings → Notifications → Liminal)
- Your phone must not be in Do Not Disturb or a Focus mode that silences Liminal
- Keep the phone charging overnight and sound turned on
- For best experience, leave the Liminal app open on the Wake screen before sleeping

### Audio cuts out when the screen locks
Background audio is enabled, but iOS can suspend audio if the session is paused for a long time or if other apps take over the audio session. Try:
- Closing other audio apps (Music, Podcasts, Spotify) before starting
- Using Airplane mode overnight to prevent interruptions from calls

### My Sankalpa recording disappeared
Sankalpa recordings are stored only on your device. If you reinstalled the app, restored from a backup that excluded app data, or cleared data from Settings → Data & Privacy, the recording will be gone. You can record a new one from the Home tab.

### How do I delete my data?
Everything lives on your device. Open Liminal → tap the gear icon on Home → **Data & Privacy** → choose what to clear. Deleting the app also removes everything.

### Does Liminal work without internet?
Yes — fully offline. All audio is bundled into the app. There are no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.
